OUR COFFEE

"As long as there is coffee in the world, how bad could things be?"

OUR LOVE FOR COFFEE! ♥

Coffee has spread all over the world and has become one of the most popular consumed beverage in the world.

At Broadway Roastery, we have been sharing our love and passion for coffee for more than 25 years. We put our heart and soul into making your favourite cup of coffee from our hand-picked fair trade coffee beans imported from all around the world. We want to share our passion for coffee with everyone in the City of Saskatoon!

Our coffee beans are carefully hand-picked by the owners of Broadway Roastery, Vince and Aileen. They pay close attention to details of the beans, wanting the beans to produce the most excellent cup of coffee with rich, complex and balanced flavours. Not only do we hand-pick which type of coffee beans we want to serve, we also select those coffee beans that are of fair trade. We support the fair trade agreement and all that it represents.

Our staff at Broadway Roastery are not only outgoing and friendly, but also take special care in the roasting process of the coffee. We roast our coffee at the time of ordering, allowing for optimization of unique flavours and aromatics to be present to you in real time.

Broadway Roastery has become one of the most well known roastery in the City of Saskatoon with their excellent blends of coffees.
