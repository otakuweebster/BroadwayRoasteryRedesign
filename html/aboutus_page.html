About Us

Our Goal

Here at the Broadway Roastery, our goal is to bring some of the world's greatest coffees home to Saskatoon. Gathering only the highest quality beans from the most exotic and well known locations, we wish to raise the standards of what is considered Good in our home city, and provide a place for Saskatoonians to gather and share in our favorite luxury. Coffee!

Our History

Founded in 1989, we joined The Broadway Business Improvement District (Broadway BID), a group of local businesses set on improving the area and giving a voice to the commercial district. Even now, the BID is working hard to make Broadway the destination choice for all things local, urban, and cool in the city.

Now, we find ourselves blessed by location. with a spot near the beautiful river, a central meeting point for the plethora of awesome local stores, and within walking distance of the local area. It has become a great place for both young and old to gather and relax surrounded by the wonderful culture of our great city, and enjoy a cop of premium quality coffee!
